Department of Commerce  

Rural Wireless Broadband for Public Safety

Goal Leaders:

Jeff Bratcher, Chief Technology and Operations Officer, performing the Executive Functions of the Chief Executive Officer
Edward Parkinson, Executive Director, External Affairs Division, performing the Non-Executive Functions of the Chief Executive Officer

Lisa Casias, Deputy Chief Executive Officer

Goal Statement: The Department of Commerce will increase public safety through a wireless broadband network by ensuring the FirstNet Solution will cover an additional 8.9 million people within the rural population, for a total of 55% with access to coverage by September 30, 2020; and by ensuring the FirstNet Solution will cover 60% of the entire rural population by September 30, 2021.
