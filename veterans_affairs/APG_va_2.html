Department of Veterans Affairs  

Community Care

Goal Leaders:

Kameron Matthews, Assistant Deputy Under Secretary for Health (ADUSH) for Community Care
Kristin Cunningham, Executive Officer to the DUSH for Community Care

Goal Statement: Improve Veterans' health outcomes and experiences by consolidating all VA-purchased care programs into one modernized community care program. By September 30, 2019, the percent of Veterans who are satisfied with receiving community care will increase from 73% (FY17Q4) to 79%.
