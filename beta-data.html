General Services Administration (GSA) Performance Data

GSA has four strategic goals - Real Estate, Acquisition, Technology, and Shared Services - in its Strategic Plan and the agency tracks progress on 28 performance indicators across those goals. This data is published in the agency’s Annual Performance Plan and Report and is now available here. Click “View More” to explore the suite of GSA’s performance measures and track their progress over time.
