Meet Our High Impact Service Providers

Working to transform the way government serves the public

CX CAP Goal and Resources

Despite some important strides to improve customer experience, many federal government services fail to meet theexpectations of the public, creating unnecessary hassle and cost for citizens, businesses, and the government itself.

The Customer Experience Cross-Agency Priority Goal and OMB Circular A-11 Section 280 begin to raise the standard of service byfirst focusing on applying proven practices to High Impact Service Providers (HISPs). These 25 services across the Federal Governmenthave been identified due to the scale and impact of their public-facing services.
