United States Agency for International Development  

Maternal and Child Health

Goal Leader: Irene Koek, USAID, Senior Deputy Assistant Administrator, Bureau for Global Health

Goal Statement: Support the global effort to prevent child and maternal deaths. By September 30, 2019, U.S. global leadership and assistance to prevent child and maternal deaths will annually reduce under-five mortality in 25 maternal and child health U.S. Government-priority countries by an average of 2 deaths per 1000 live births per year as compared to 2017.
