Goal Leader: Pending submission by agency

Goal Statement: Expand public access to lands and waters administered by the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, as prescribed by Secretarial Orders 3356 and 3366, for hunting, fishing, and other forms of outdoor recreation so more Americans can create inspiring and lasting memories. By September 30, 2020, the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service will expand hunting and fishing opportunities on 2 million acres of National Wildlife Refuges and National Fish Hatcheries, and open or expand an estimated 700 opportunities.
