Goal Statement: Increase the available water supply in the Western States through conservation-related programs to help provide a more sustainable and secure water supply, reducing the impact of drought for the benefit of the public and the economy. By September 30, 2020, the Bureau of Reclamation will facilitate water conservation capacity of an additional 32,108 acre-feet to help reduce the impact of drought.
