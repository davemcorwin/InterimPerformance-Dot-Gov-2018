<h3>Job Creation: Apprenticeship Expansion</h3>
<p><b>Goal Leader: </b>John Pallasch, Assistant Secretary</p>
<p><b>Goal Statement:</b> Enroll 1 million new apprentices (including registered programs, industry-recognized apprenticeships, and other non-registered programs) over the next five years to enable more Americans to obtain jobs that pay a family-sustaining wage through high quality earn-and-learn opportunities.  By September 30, 2019, DOL will enroll 280,000 new apprentices as part of the agency’s broader efforts to promote and expand apprenticeship. </p>

<h3>Worker Safety: Reduce Miner Injuries</h3>
