Reduce the Average Length of Homelessness

Goal Leader: David C. Woll, Jr., Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary for Community Planning and Development

Goal Statement: HUD will reduce the average length of homelessness in communities by an average of three days from FY 2018 to the end of FY 2020.
