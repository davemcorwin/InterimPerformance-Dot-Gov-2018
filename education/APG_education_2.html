Department of Education  

{{page.title}}

Goal Leader: Scott Stump, Assistant Secretary for Career, Technical, and Adult Education, Office of Career, Technical, and Adult Education (OCTAE)

Goal Statement: Improve nationwide awareness of and access to career pathways that support job skills development and career readiness. By September 30, 2021, through programs such as the Career and Technical Education and Adult Education State Grants, the Department will: (1) support the creation and expansion of integrated education and training (IET) programs in all 50 States, Puerto Rico and the District of Columbia; (2) support the enrollment of 70,000 participants in IET programs; (3) increase enrollment of Career and Technical Education concentrators in science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) fields by 10 percent; and (4) increase the number of federal financial aid recipients who earn a postsecondary credential in STEM by 25,000.
