Provide Regulatory Relief to Education Stakeholders

Goal Leader:

Robert Eitel, Senior Counselor to Secretary

Deputy Goal Leader: Elizabeth McFadden, Deputy General Counsel


Goal Statement: By September 30, 2019, the Department will reduce the regulatory burden on education stakeholders by submitting to OMB no less than 25 deregulatory actions (against a baseline of zero (0) for FY 2015 and 2016).
