{{ page.title }}

Goal Leader: Dr. Charles Verdon, NNSA, Deputy Administrator for Defense Programse

Goal Statement: Maintain and modernize the U.S. nuclear weapons stockpile and dismantle excess nuclear weapons to meet the national security requirements, as assigned by the President, through the Nuclear Posture Review (NPR). DOE will:

  • By Sept. 30, 2018, complete at least 90% of W76-1 production unit builds and achieve B61-12 system final design review
  • By Sept. 30, 2019, achieve 100% of W76-1 production unit builds, and update the plan for future life extension programs (LEPs) in NNSA strategic planning documents as outlined in the NPR.

December 2019.
