Goal Leaders:

Conner Prochaska, Chief Commercialization Officer, U.S. Department of Energy
Marcos Gonzales Harsha, Principal Deputy Director, Office of Technology Transitions

Goal Statement: Enable increased commercial adoption and use of the broad suite of technologies and facilities developed and maintained by the Department of Energy through enhanced public-private and public-public partnerships. By September 30, 2021, expand engagement efforts to private and other federal entities and create tailored opportunities and products to simplify access to DOE's portfolio of facilities, technologies, and world-leading experts.
