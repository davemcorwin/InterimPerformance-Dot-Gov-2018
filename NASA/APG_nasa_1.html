National Aeronautics and Space Administration  

{{page.title}}

Goal Leader: Ken Bowersox, Deputy Associate Administrator, Human Exploration and Operations Mission Directorate (HEOMD)

Goal Statement: Enable a robust commercial low Earth orbit economy in which transportation, habitation, and on-orbit services are available for purchase by NASA and other customers. By September 30, 2021, NASA will support the development of commercial services, including through releasing new business opportunities, supporting demonstration flights, beginning certification activities, and demonstrating commercial capabilities.
